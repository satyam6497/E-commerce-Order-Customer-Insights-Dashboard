# 📊 E-commerce Order Fulfillment & Customer Behavior Analysis

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project analyzes an e-commerce dataset to understand order lifecycle, delivery performance, and customer behavior. The goal is to derive actionable business insights and identify areas for improvement, especially in customer retention and operational efficiency.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze order fulfillment performance
* Evaluate delivery efficiency
* Understand customer behavior (repeat vs one-time users)
* Identify key revenue-driving factors

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

* ~97% of orders are successfully delivered, indicating strong fulfillment performance
* Average delivery time is ~12.5 days
* Only ~3% of customers are repeat users, highlighting low customer retention
* Revenue is concentrated in a few product categories
* Majority of customers are one-time buyers
